Burger Recipe

A burger is a popular fast food dish typically consisting of a cooked ground beef patty placed inside a soft bun. It is often topped with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup or mustard. Variations of the burger include different proteins such as chicken, turkey, or plant-based patties. Known for its versatility, the burger can be customized to suit a wide range of tastes and preferences.

Ingredients:

  • Bun
  • Cheese
  • Onions and tomatoes
  • patty(veg patty is banned)

Steps to prepare:

  1. Season ground beef (or your choice of protein) and shape it into a patty. Cook it on a grill or pan to your desired doneness.
  2. Lightly toast the burger buns on a grill or in a pan for extra flavor and texture.
  3. Place the cooked patty on the bottom bun and add toppings like cheese, lettuce, tomato, onions,and pickles.
  4. Top with the second bun, press lightly, and serve with your favorite sides.
