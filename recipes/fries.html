Fries Recipe

Fries, also known as French fries, are thinly sliced potatoes that are deep-fried until golden and crispy. They are often seasoned with salt and can be served as a side dish or snack. Popular worldwide, fries are commonly paired with burgers, sandwiches, or dips like ketchup or mayonnaise. Variations include curly fries, sweet potato fries, and loaded fries with toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Potatos
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil

Steps to prepare:

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Rinse and pat dry to remove excess starch.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or fryer to 350°F.
  4. Fry potatoes until golden and crispy, then drain and season with salt.
